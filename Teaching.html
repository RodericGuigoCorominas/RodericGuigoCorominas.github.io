School of Molecular and Theorerical Biology

The School of Molecular and Theoretical Biology is aimed for highschool students interested in science in general and specifically in Biology. Students combine small biology-realted projects with other free-topic courses. This is some material from past courses:

  • Easy Nets (Summer 2021). An introduction to neural networks and convolutions through the basics of multivariate function calculus in Python.
    • Course Repository.
  • Topological Data Analysis in Python (Summer 2019). Topological understanding of persistent homology and applications in Python.
    • Course Repository.
  • Fractals, Chaos and Dynamical Systems (Summer 2016). Complex dynamical systems using Python. From iterative maps to plots of Mandelbrot and Julia sets.
